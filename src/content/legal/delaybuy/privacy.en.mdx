# DelayBuy Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: January 22, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to DelayBuy! We understand the importance of your privacy, which is why we have created this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

DelayBuy is an intelligent delayed purchasing assistant app designed to help users make rational consumption decisions. We are committed to protecting your privacy rights and ensuring your personal information is handled securely.

## Information Collection

### Types of Information We Collect

**Account Information**
- Username, email address (only when using premium features)
- Account creation time and last login time

**App Usage Data**
- Product information you add (product name, price, delay period, notes, images)
- Your consumption decision records (purchase/not purchase decisions and reasons)
- App usage statistics (feature usage frequency, usage duration, etc.)

**Device Information**
- Device model, operating system version
- App version number
- Device unique identifiers (for data synchronization)

**Analytics Data**
- App crash reports
- Performance data
- User behavior analytics (anonymized)

### How We Collect Information

- **Direct Collection**: Information you actively input
- **Automatic Collection**: Data automatically generated during app usage
- **Third-party Services**: Analytics data collected through Firebase, RevenueCat, and other third-party services

## Information Usage

### Usage Purposes

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:

**Core Service Functions**
- Providing delayed purchasing functionality
- Sending decision reminder notifications
- Generating consumption statistics and analysis reports
- Data synchronization and backup

**Service Improvement**
- Analyzing app usage to optimize user experience
- Fixing technical issues and improving app performance
- Developing new features and improving existing ones

**Customer Support**
- Responding to your inquiries and feedback
- Providing technical support and troubleshooting

**Legal Compliance**
- Complying with applicable laws and regulations
- Protecting our legitimate interests

### Legal Basis for Data Processing

- **Contract Performance**: Processing necessary information to provide DelayBuy services
- **Legitimate Interest**: Improving service quality and user experience
- **User Consent**: Processing specific information with your explicit consent

## Information Sharing

### We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing Circumstances

We only share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

**Service Providers**
- Firebase (data storage and analytics)
- RevenueCat (subscription management)
- Apple/Google (app store services)

**Legal Requirements**
- As required by law or government agencies
- To protect our legitimate interests
- To prevent fraud or security threats

**Business Transfers**
- Your information may be transferred in case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

## Data Security

### Security Measures

We implement multiple security measures to protect your information:

- **Encrypted Transmission**: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
- **Data Encryption**: Sensitive information is encrypted when stored
- **Access Control**: Strict restrictions on access to your information
- **Regular Audits**: Regular review and updates of security measures

### Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Some data is stored on your device
- **Cloud Storage**: Data is stored using secure cloud services like Firebase
- **Data Backup**: Regular backup of important data

## Your Rights

### Access and Correction

You can view and correct your personal information at any time.

### Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and related data.

### Data Export

You can export your data, including product lists and decision records.

### Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for specific data processing at any time.

## Children's Privacy

DelayBuy is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We will ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

## Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified through in-app notifications or email. Continued use of the service indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
