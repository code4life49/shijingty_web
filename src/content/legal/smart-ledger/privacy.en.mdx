# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** September 1, 2025  
**Last Updated:** September 1, 2025

---

## Overview

ShijingTY Technologies (referred to as "we," "us," or "our") develops the "Smart Ledger" application (referred to as "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy rights. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

We adhere to **privacy-first** design principles, collecting only the minimum data necessary to provide core functionality. We never engage in advertising tracking or data sales.

---

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

- **App Diagnostics**: Crash logs, performance metrics, error reports
- **Device Information**: OS version, app version, device model (for compatibility optimization)
- **Usage Statistics**: Feature usage frequency, session duration (anonymized)

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address if you choose to create an account
- **Sync Data**: Your accounting data if you enable cloud synchronization (encrypted in transit and storage)
- **Feedback**: Content you submit through in-app feedback features

### Information We Do Not Collect

- Precise location information
- Contacts or SMS content
- Usage data from other apps
- Personal profiles for advertising purposes

---

## How We Use Information

We use collected information solely for the following legitimate purposes:

### Core Functionality

- Ensure proper app operation and data synchronization
- Provide personalized accounting experience
- Process your account requests and technical support

### Product Improvement

- Analyze app performance and stability
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Develop new features and improve user experience

### Security

- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Protect user data security
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

---

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We strictly limit information sharing and may only share your information in the following circumstances:

### Service Providers

- **Cloud Storage Services**: For data sync functionality (such as iCloud, Google Drive)
- **Analytics Services**: Using only anonymized data for app performance analysis

### Legal Requirements

- Comply with applicable laws, regulations, or government requests
- Protect our legitimate interests or user safety
- Respond to emergency situations

### What We Never Do

- ❌ Sell personal data to advertisers
- ❌ Share user profiles with social media platforms
- ❌ Engage in data trading for marketing purposes

---

## Data Security Measures

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encrypted Transmission**: All data transmission uses TLS/SSL encryption
- **Local Encryption**: Sensitive data is encrypted locally on your device
- **Access Control**: Strict limitations on employee access to user data
- **Regular Audits**: Periodic security vulnerability assessments and remediation

---

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Control Rights

- **View Data**: You can view information we've collected about you at any time
- **Correct Data**: You can request corrections if you find inaccurate information
- **Delete Data**: You can request deletion of your personal information (except where legally required to retain)
- **Data Export**: You can request your data in common formats

### Feature Controls

- **Disable Sync**: You can disable cloud synchronization at any time
- **Limit Analytics**: You can turn off usage data analytics in settings
- **Account Deletion**: You can delete your account and associated data at any time

### How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us to exercise your rights through:

- Email: privacy@shijingty.com
- In-app feedback feature
- We will respond to your requests within 7 business days

---

## Data Retention Policy

- **Account Data**: Completely removed within 30 days of account deletion
- **Sync Data**: Cloud data can be deleted at any time; local data is under your control
- **Log Data**: Automatically collected logs retained for a maximum of 90 days
- **Feedback Data**: Used for product improvement, retained for no more than 2 years
